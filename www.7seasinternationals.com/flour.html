  1. All-Purpose Flour: All-purpose flour is a versatile type of flour suitable for a wide variety of culinary applications. It is a staple in baking and cooking, used to make everything from bread and cakes to cookies and pancakes. Our all-purpose flour is finely milled and offers a balanced blend of protein and starch, making it a reliable choice for everyday cooking needs.

  2. Whole Wheat Flour: Whole wheat flour is milled from the entire wheat kernel, including the bran and germ, making it more nutritious than refined flours. It retains more fiber, vitamins, and minerals, providing a healthier alternative for those seeking whole-grain options in their recipes.

  3. Bread Flour: Bread flour is specifically designed for yeast-based bread-making. It has a higher protein content than all-purpose flour, which gives bread its structure and chewy texture. Our bread flour is ideal for producing crusty artisan loaves and soft sandwich bread.

  4. Cake Flour: Cake flour is finely milled and has a lower protein content than all-purpose flour, making it perfect for tender and delicate baked goods. It is commonly used in cakes, pastries, and biscuits, providing a light and airy texture.

  5. Gluten-Free Flour: Our gluten-free flour blend caters to those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. It is a combination of various gluten-free flours and starches, providing a suitable substitute for traditional wheat-based flours without compromising on taste and texture.
